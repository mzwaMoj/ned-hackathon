## SAVINGS ACCOUNTS

### 1. Essential Savings Account
**Product Code:** SAV-001
**Description:** Basic savings account for everyday banking needs

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Minimum Age: 18 years
- Income Requirement: None
- Credit Score: Not required
- Employment Status: Any (students, employed, self-employed, unemployed)

**Key Features:**
- Minimum Opening Deposit: $25
- Minimum Monthly Balance: $100
- Interest Rate: 0.50% APY
- Monthly Maintenance Fee: $5 (waived if balance > $500)

## HOME LOANS

### 11. First-Time Homebuyer Mortgage
**Product Code:** MTG-001
**Description:** Special mortgage program for first-time homebuyers

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Minimum Age: 21 years
- Income Requirement: $40,000 annually (individual) or $60,000 (household)
- Credit Score: 620+
- Employment Status: Employed for at least 2 years or Self-employed for 3 years
- Debt-to-Income Ratio: Must be below 43%
- Must complete homebuyer education course
- Cannot have owned home in past 3 years

**Key Features:**
- Loan Amount: Up to $400,000
- Down Payment: As low as 3%
- Loan Term: 15 or 30 years
- Interest Rate: 6.25% - 7.50% (30-year fixed)
- PMI Required: Yes, if down payment < 20%
- Closing Cost Assistance: Up to $5,000 grant available
- Property Type: Primary residence only
- Origination Fee: 1%

**Target Audience:** First-time homebuyers, young families

---

## SPECIALTY ACCOUNTS

### 19. Health Savings Account (HSA)
**Product Code:** HSA-001
**Description:** Tax-advantaged savings account for medical expenses

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Must be enrolled in High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP)
- Cannot be claimed as dependent on someone else's tax return
- Not enrolled in Medicare
- No other health coverage (with exceptions)

**Key Features:**
- Minimum Opening Deposit: $0
- Annual Contribution Limits (2025):
  - Individual: $4,300
  - Family: $8,550
  - Age 55+ catch-up: Additional $1,000
- Interest Rate: 2.50% APY
- Monthly Maintenance Fee: $0
- Investment Options: Available for balances over $2,000
- Tax Benefits: Triple tax advantage (deductible contributions, tax-free growth, tax-free withdrawals for qualified expenses)
- Rollover: Funds never expire
- Debit Card: Free HSA debit card

**Target Audience:** Individuals/families with HDHPs seeking tax-advantaged medical savings
